About Startup Company

Startup Company is a business simulation sandbox game. You play as the CEO of a newly founded software development company looking to earn a name for yourself in the industry.

Startup Company includes:

  • Build Mode: Design your own office
  • Contract Work: Win contracts by competing on price and service
  • Crafting System: Merge components together to create modules. Modules creates products.
  • Products: Update your product's features and generate hype to increase your user-base and income for your product.
  • Languages: The game is currently available in English, German, French, Spanish, Brazilian Portuguese and Simplified Chinese.

Hire employees

A great company is built by great people. Optimize your benefits and work conditions to attract the best employees.

Win contracts

Get ready to have your people get in touch with their people!

You'll be facing hundreds of available contracts, but not all of them will be good for your developers. Work your way up and compete with the best in the industry to win the big contracts!

Develop, merge, deliver!

That's right, even software needs a little piecing together!

No matter if you want to deliver contracts or build your own product you need components and modules. Use developers, designers and lead developers to produce what you need.

Startup Company includes a fully featured crafting/development system where you will be able to merge components into modules.

Release, update and expand

Your work doesn't stop once your product is released into the world!

Update your product's features and generate hype to increase your user-base and income for your product.

You'll have pay attention to your hype and user count as well as your server load for your products. Keeping the product under constant development and releasing new version is key to success.

It's your sandbox, let your imagination run wild!

Design the perfect office

What's an office without some desks and a bubbling water cooler?

Startup Company features a full blown sandbox mode, enabling you to design your dream office. Do it well and your developers will be working faster than ever!
